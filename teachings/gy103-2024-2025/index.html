Contemporary Europe (GY 103)

Graduate Teaching Assistant for Block A: The Political Economy of European Integration. Taught 3 seminar groups across 9 classes in Autumn Term 2024/25 at LSE. Recipient of the LSE Class Teacher Award 2024 and 2025.

Course: GY 103 – Contemporary Europe     Term: Autumn Term 2024/25     Role: Graduate Teaching Assistant

This course covers the economics and politics of European integration. As class teacher, I was responsible for 3 seminar groups running alongside the main lecture series. Classes consolidated lecture content through student-led debates, reading card exercises, and regional policy pitches. Recipient of the LSE Class Teacher Award 2024 and 2025.

Debate Topics

  • Debate 1: Has economic integration improved competitiveness and boosted economic growth in the European Union?
  • Debate 2: Has economic integration been a driver of increased regional inequality in the European Union?
