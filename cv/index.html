Professional Summary

Researcher and policy analyst with 7+ years of experience at the intersection of economic development, geospatial analysis, and quantitative impact evaluation. 5+ years of experience in research, advisory, and project management in international development cooperation, contributing to entrepreneurship, private sector development, and diaspora finance initiatives across Europe and Sub-Saharan Africa. 5+ years of hands-on work with technical writing, data visualisation, geospatial analysis, satellite data, and econometrics to inform local development strategies, policy design, and impact evaluation. Fluent in German, English, and French, with strong technical skills in R, STATA, Google Earth Engine.
