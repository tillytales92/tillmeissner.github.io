This report was produced during an internship at the **African Center for Economic Transformation (ACET)** in Accra, Ghana (2016), contributing to ACET's Policy Advisory section. It examines China's engagement in African development with a focus on trade, finance, and official development assistance (ODA), and applies ACET's **African Transformation Index (ATI)** methodology to assess economic transformation across 30 African economies.

The analysis fed into the **African Transformation Report 2017**, ACET's flagship publication tracking structural economic change across the continent.
