## Course Structure

**Block A – The Political Economy of European Integration (Weeks 2–11)**

| Week | Dates | Topic | Activity |
|------|-------|-------|----------|
| 2 | 07–13 Oct | History, Facts, Law & Institutions of European Integration | Intro class |
| 3 | 14–20 Oct | Economics of Preferential Liberalisation & Market Size Effects | Reading card 1 |
| 4 | 21–27 Oct | Growth Effects and Factor Market Integration | Reading card 2 |
| 5 | 28 Oct–3 Nov | Location Effects, Economic Geography and Regional Policy | Reading card 3 · **Debate 1** |
| 6 | 4–10 Nov | *Reading week* | Essay 1 due (Fri 8 Nov) |
| 7 | 11–17 Nov | The EU Budget | **Debate 2** |
| 8 | 18–24 Nov | EU Cohesion Policy: Regional Policy (10.2–10.7) | Reading card 4 |

## Debate Topics

- **Debate 1:** Has economic integration improved competitiveness and boosted economic growth in the European Union?
- **Debate 2:** Has economic integration been a driver of increased regional inequality in the European Union?
